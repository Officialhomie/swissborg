Frequently asked questions

In short, what is thematic investing?

SwissBorg Thematics offers a unique investment approach, grouping the most promising crypto assets by themes to strengthen your portfolio. With just one tap, you're investing with SwissBorg's experts and our advanced algorithms in your team, ensuring you always have the best-selected tokens and a consistently updated investment.

What is rebalancing?

Your Thematic consists of various tokens/coins, each with a defined target percentage in line with their risk and reward potential. Because of cryptoâ€™s volatility, these percentages can easily drift away. To maintain the desired target percentage of each coin, necessary exchanges are therefore made to rebalance asset allocations. Rebalancing automatically takes profit from outperformers & accumulates tokens at lower price points. More here.

What is reallocation?

For your portfolio to remain relevant & well diversified in fast-changing markets, SwissBorg reviews continuously the assets within its Thematics. We evaluate new opportunities and change asset composition & allocations periodically to ensure that each Thematic evolves true to its theme & performs at its optimal level.

Do I really own the tokens/coins?

Yes, with Thematics you own all the underlying assets. It is a bundle of tokens, contrary to an ETF or an index.

What is the minimum investment amount?

The minimum investment amount for the Golden Thematic is 40 EUR or the equivalent in your local currency. The minimum investment amount for the Best Blockchains Thematic and the Gaming Thematic is 50 EUR or the equivalent in your local currency. The minimum investment amount for the Banter Bundle is 250 EUR or the equivalent in your local currency.

What is the minimum investment period?

There is no minimum investment period. You are free to sell your Thematic investment at any time. In the future, you will be able to earn rewards based on your holding duration.
